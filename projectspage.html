Lego Stop Motion


The folowing video is a short film I created for an Enterprise Course. The project required that each student create a 5 minute video explaining a particuar topic that was taught in class. The catch was that it had to be done in a form of storytelling. For example a topic like security could be explained through a story about a princess in her castle. In this story, a father explains to his kids the Just In Time inventory model using Santa Claus as an example.
